50S

Product Centric Marketing

All what we talked about was marketing as natural behaviour with no actual science basics, and the first marketing science starts in 50s, marketing 1.0, the first generation of marketing, with the concept of selling and selling and selling, as it was product centric marketing, and it was when E.J. McCarthy invented the 4 Ps concept. Product, price, promotion & Place, the right product at the right place with the right price & promotion. Everything has to be “the right” thing as he is just convincing the upper layer in the human brain, the cortex, so the logic is his main power to make people buy his product.
The real Science of Marketing

70S

Customer oriented marketing

The second generation appeared in the 70s, when marketing was updated from marketing 1.0 into marketing 2.0, instead of caring about the product, it start caring about the customer, how to impress him and gain his loyalty for a long period of time, and that’s why he was working on the 2nd layer inside the brain, the limbic layer, this layer is affected by emotions, the customer buys the product because he loves the brand, so a methodology was invented to apply this theory which was “segmentation, targeting & positioning”.
It means choosing a category of people from the whole mass and affect them only while positioning the brand in their head.
Another direction belonging to generation 2.0 is the “branding” concept, when the product has a very big effect on the customers that every product in this field is named after it, just like cola, we call this name on any type of soda.
In this two generations, advertising was a big deal of marketing, whether the advertising was about a product or caring about the customer, especially in the presence of Tv, radio, magazines & newspaper, so the brands got the chance to appear everywhere for the customer, and tell them about the product and the brand, and how they will benefit out of it.

90S

Value Driven marketing

In the late 90s the great marketing 3.0 took place .. based on the Steve Jobs’ concept “make the world a better place” .. we - as a marketer - aren’t customer or product centric .. we have a bigger aim in making the world a better place and you will know how later on.This marketing generation starts to dig deeper in the brain, so he gets out of caring about product or customer to be about the value produced from marketing, where it affects the customers beliefs, not just logic or emotions. It appeared in the 90s which means the 1st appearance of the internet too, that made a huge change in people’s life, and other changes in the technology that helped people to skip ads which appears everywhere, and we can’ see this obviously in the number of people watching TV that is decreased while people watching movies in cinema or on computers increases, like people stopped listening to the radio and start using the mp3, mp4 & now their smart phone. and thanks to the social media that make the customer has to say his opinion, and the relationship between the product and customer is 2 ways direction, he's now not just a consumer, he's a customer with opinions and beliefs.

Everything has a methodology, and inbound marketing can be applied through 4 steps.

Attract

You know that you may pass by the same person everyday and you don’t notice that, neither do him, and that’s simply because you don’t know each other, just like the the chinese people that you can’t recognize them from each other, and that leads us to the importance of 1st step in inbound methodology, just to attract them and let them know you, and in this stage everything is related to content.
But what content means?
The content may be a photo or an article, or a video or whatever, it’s any content you can produce to add a value to the customer, not just any value but a specific one related to your business and your buyer persona
How & Where to publish this content ?
1- Blogging: let’s assume that you work in shoe business, then you should blog about this field, you should blog about your experience in this field, advise them about what they should know, or you may tell them from your daily situation, just help them and add value to them through blogging.
2- Social Media: the most powerful channel that connect you with people, as it’s a 2 ways direction channel, where you listen more than you talk, and if you really added a value, social media has the power of sharing, where you can’t hide.
3- Website: if you have a website then you have an online store, you can introduce yourself, your products, and everything, a website is considered your view, it represents you work, so take care how you want your work to look like.
4- SEO: the importance of SEO ranking can’t be explained as it’s so obvious, google is number 1 helper for anyone searching for anything, so if you don’t pick your place at google, you are missing lots of people searching for your value, and the content you write can be SEO optimized with the same value you are providing on your several channels.

Convert

Since your buyer persona now knows that your are his value station whenever he needs anything in your field, but what if you need to send him a specific content personalised to him, here we come to the contact part, how can you get the buyer persona contact, how can you transfer him from just a visitor into a lead.
Of course you will use content, but a content that needs access, how would a content with an access help in that ?
first:
you will introduce a value just like what you content must do, but in this case it’s a downloadable value that needs access
Second:
type of content that needs access may be an ebook, webinar,white paper or other
Third:
you’re going to make him to fill in a form of data you are introducing to him, this data must be the data you need to know about him, not just any data, you need his contact, you may need to know the type of his work and where he works, as this data will help you to make more personalised content further on. Never ask him a question that you won’t need, this will put you in the risk that he won’t feel that he really needs this value for all this data, at the same time this data you don’t really need.

Close

you rule is big in making the world a better place, and this is the main objective of inbound marketing. But how will this affect you ? simply because you will never master your field until this is your big mission and big objective, other than this many others in your field can cross you.

Delight

your customer has bought what he needed, and he’s gone, you can’t just leave him like this, you should make him feel that you are not just nurturing him to buy, let’s imagine if he bought and left, there are 2 options:
1- he will be happy with the product and forget you by time.
2- he will be so happy that he’s talking about you in crowds and among his friends, and that’s what you seek for.
So now you are still nurturing the customer, and this can be done by several perspectives:
1- you can talk about him and his success story with you and your product.
2- provide him intellectual gifts that would add value to him and help you to be a part of his life.
3- applying the same concept of lead nurturing but here we are going to name it customer nurturing.
